# Mirra 👁️

**Know your competitors better than they know themselves.**

Mirra is a competitive intelligence tool that turns any competitor's website into a full breakdown — their weaknesses, what their customers are complaining about, and exactly how to beat them.
 

## What it does

Paste a competitor's URL. In about 15 seconds, Mirra gives you:

- **Their real customer complaints** — pulled from reviews across the web, not made up
- **A messaging brief** — ready-to-use copy you can deploy today
- **A priority action list** — ranked by what will actually move the needle
- **An executive summary** — strengths, weaknesses, and a clear verdict
- **Momentum signals** — whether they're growing, plateauing, or slipping

Everything is organized into a clean, step-by-step report so you can move through the intel without getting lost.

 
## Who it's for

- Founders who want to know where competitors are dropping the ball
- Marketers looking for an honest angle in a crowded market
- Sales teams who need to handle objections before they come up
- Anyone tired of guessing what makes them different

 

## How it works

1. You enter a competitor's URL
2. Mirra scans their site, pulls customer reviews, and reads market signals
3. You get a full intelligence report — broken into 5 focused pages

No spreadsheets. No analyst. No guessing.
 

## The report is split into 5 pages

| # | Section | What you get |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Beat Them Brief | Headline, offer angle, CTA, and where to find their frustrated customers |
| 2 | Customer Ammunition | Real complaint patterns with suggested angles to counter them |
| 3 | Priority Actions | What to do first, second, and third — with effort and impact ratings |
| 4 | Executive Summary | Strengths, weaknesses, and a one-line verdict |
| 5 | Momentum Signals | Whether they're on the way up, leveling off, or starting to slip |



> *Your competitors are hiding something. Mirra sees it.*
